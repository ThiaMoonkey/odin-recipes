Thin Crusted Pizza

This is my favorite homemade pizza, Its simply and delicious with a nice thin crust. This recipe will mostly be for the dough but I will add the topping I prefer to use. I will also be providing a quick garlic butter recipe at the end to enjoy your pizza with.

  1. Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water that's 110 degrees F (44 degrees C) in a small bowl. Let stand until yeast softens and begins to form a creamy foam, about 5 to 8 minutes.
  2. Combine bread flour, olive oil, sugar, salt, and yeast mixture in a mixing bowl; stir well to combine. Beat until a stiff dough has formed. Cover the bowl; let rise at room temperature until dough doubles in volume, about 30 minutes.
  3. Transfer dough to a floured surface and knead until smooth, about 2 minutes. Add remaining 1/4 cup flour if dough is too sticky.
  4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  5. Turn dough out onto a well-floured surface. Form dough into a round and roll out into a thin crust(I like to brush the dough with garlic oil but you can use olive or skip this part). Cover with your favorite sauce and toppings.
  6. Bake pizza in the preheated oven until cheese is bubbly and crust is golden brown, about 20 minutes.

Garlic butter dipping sauce

You'll need:

  • butter
  • garlic powder
  • salt(optional)
  1. Melt butter in a small saucepan over low heat.
  2. Once the butter is completely melted, remove from heat and use a spoon to carefully remove the foamy layer on top; discarding foam.
  3. Whisk in garlic powder and salt, if using.
  4. Transfer to individual dipping bowls.
