My go-to Lasagna

This delicious recipe is my go-to Lasagna, it is a family favorite, Lasagna dish I crave at least twice a year!

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over what you need before cooking:

Ingredients you'll need:

  • Two types of meat: Lean and Italian sausage
  • Onion and Garlic to cook with the meat
  • one canned Tomatoes; two cans Tomato Sauce; and two cans Tomato Paste
  • White Sugar
  • Spices & Seasoning: parsley; dried basil; salt; Italian seasoning; fennel seeds; and black pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles: Homemade or Bought
  • Cheeses: Parmesan; Mozzarella; and Ricotta
  • Egg: And lastly an egg to help bind the ricotta cheese
