Protein Oatmeal

Ingredients you'll need

  • Eggs: One egg
  • Milk: 1tbsp milk; 1 cup of Milk
  • Vanilla extract: ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Spices: ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon, 1 pinch ground nutmeg,
  • Salt: 1 pinch salt
  • Oats: ½ cup rolled oats
  • Syrup:1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • Peanut Butter: 1 tablespoon peanut butter, or to taste
  • Banana: 1 banana, sliced or ½ banana, sliced

Directions

  1. Whisk egg with 1 tablespoon milk in a small bowl. Set aside.
  2. Bring 1 cup milk, vanilla extract, cinnamon, salt, and nutmeg to a boil in a saucepan over medium-low heat, stirring often. Mix in oats and cook until thickened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Pour beaten egg mixture slowly into the oats, stirring constantly. Continue cooking until egg is fully cooked and oatmeal is very thick and creamy, about 5 minutes. Add additional milk if oatmeal gets too thick.
  4. Pour oatmeal into a bowl; mix in maple syrup and peanut butter. Top with banana slices or whatever fruit you'd prefer, your choice of nuts and more cinnamon.

Cook's Note | This can be prepared the night before. Make the oatmeal, pour it into a heat- safe container, and store in the fridge. When ready to eat, microwave oatmeal until warm, may need extra milk to soften.
